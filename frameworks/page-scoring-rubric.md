# Page Scoring Rubric

Score each strategic page from 0 to 5 across SEO, AEO, and GEO.

## SEO Score

- 0: Not indexable or no clear search intent.
- 1: Indexed but weak targeting and structure.
- 2: Basic keyword targeting and metadata.
- 3: Strong intent match, headings, internal links, and adequate depth.
- 4: Competitive content depth, technical health, schema, and strong internal links.
- 5: Category-leading page with topical authority, backlinks, and strong SERP performance.

## AEO Score

- 0: No extractable answer.
- 1: Some relevant content, but buried or ambiguous.
- 2: Basic FAQ or heading structure.
- 3: Clear answer blocks, definitions, and question-led sections.
- 4: Strong tables, FAQs, schema, summaries, and concise answer language.
- 5: Best-in-class extractable page for answer engines and AI Overviews.

## GEO Score

- 0: No trust or recommendation signals.
- 1: Brand/product present but thin or unverified.
- 2: Some product data and owned claims.
- 3: Reviews, specs, entity clarity, and internal corroboration.
- 4: Third-party validation, fresh data, comparison context, and clear differentiators.
- 5: Highly cite-worthy page with external trust, original data, and strong recommendation logic.

## Notes

Use this rubric before and after optimization. Store before/after scores with the page URL, date, and evidence.
