# AI Visibility Tracking

AI visibility should be measured with fixed prompt sets and repeatable evidence collection.

## Surfaces

- Google AI Overviews
- Google AI Mode
- ChatGPT search/browsing experiences
- Perplexity
- Gemini
- Copilot

## Prompt Categories

- best product for use case
- X vs Y comparison
- price-constrained shopping
- local/regional product discovery
- "what is" definitions
- how-to guidance
- category education
- brand comparison
- gift recommendation
- technical buying advice

## Metrics

- brand mentioned
- product mentioned
- URL cited
- citation source
- answer position
- recommendation strength
- competitor mentions
- sentiment
- stated reasons for recommendation
- missing information

## Cadence

- Weekly for priority prompts.
- Monthly for broader category prompts.
- After each major content update for target pages.
