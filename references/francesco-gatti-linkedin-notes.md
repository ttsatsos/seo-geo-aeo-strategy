# Francesco Gatti LinkedIn Notes

These notes summarize saved LinkedIn-style infographics and should be treated as ideas to test, not definitive ranking doctrine.

## 3 Layers of AI Before Recommendation

Concept:

- SEO helps systems find the brand.
- AEO helps systems extract answers from content.
- GEO helps systems verify, cite, and recommend the brand.

Useful translation:

- SEO = discoverability
- AEO = extractability
- GEO = recommendability

Operational takeaway:

Pages should be scored across all three dimensions, not only traditional keyword rank.

## Ecommerce Comparison Page Formats

Six formats worth prioritizing:

1. X vs Y head-to-head
2. Best X for use case
3. Feature, ingredient, spec, or material matrix
4. Price-tier comparison
5. When to choose A over B
6. Customer review roundup

Operational takeaway:

Comparison pages are especially useful for AI-mediated shopping because they provide named entities, structured attributes, decision rules, and evidence.

## Product Ranked by ChatGPT Checklist

Ten areas to fix:

1. Product-first data
2. CITE framework: complete, identifiable, trusted, extractable
3. PDP optimization
4. Author/founder authority
5. Fresh catalog data
6. Multi-format content
7. Off-site trust
8. Original research
9. GEO audit
10. AI share-of-voice tracking

Operational takeaway:

AI shopping visibility depends on product clarity, structured data, fresh facts, trust signals, and repeatable measurement.
