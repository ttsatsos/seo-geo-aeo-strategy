# GEO Audit Prompt

Use this to manually or semi-automatically test how AI systems answer buyer prompts.

```text
Run a GEO audit for this prompt:

[PROMPT]

Brand:
[BRAND]

Competitors:
[COMPETITORS]

Record:
1. Which brands/products are recommended?
2. Is our brand mentioned?
3. Is our brand cited?
4. What sources are cited?
5. What reasons are given for each recommendation?
6. What information appears to be missing from our owned site?
7. What off-site trust signals appear to influence the answer?
8. What page/content should we create or improve?
9. What schema/product data should be added?
10. Priority score from 1-5.
```
