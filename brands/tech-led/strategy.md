# Tech-led.com Search Strategy

## Brand Context

Tech-led.com is an industrial and research LED distributor for the Marubeni LED line. The catalog spans UV through SWIR (365nm to 1750nm) and serves B2B technical buyers: design engineers, university researchers, medical device OEMs, machine vision integrators, semiconductor inspection teams, fNIRS researchers, and multispectral imaging system designers. The store is WooCommerce-based with thousands of SKUs.

Tech-led.com is not a consumer lighting brand, video wall integrator, or digital signage vendor. Strategy should treat it as a technical components supplier.

## Strategic Goal

Make Tech-led.com easy for search engines and AI systems to find, understand, trust, and recommend for industrial and research-grade LED selection by wavelength, package, and application.

## Priority Themes

- wavelength-specific LEDs across UV, visible, NIR, and SWIR (365, 405, 450, 660, 780, 850, 940, 1050, 1200, 1450, 1550, 1650, 1750 nm)
- UV LED pillar content (curing, fluorescence, wafer/semiconductor inspection)
- NIR LED pillar content (night vision, biomedical, proximity sensing)
- SWIR LED pillar content (moisture/water sensing, hyperspectral imaging, food and agricultural inspection)
- multispectral and multi-wavelength LED arrays / modules
- application clusters: medical and biomedical, fNIRS, pulse oximetry, hyperspectral imaging, machine vision, security and night vision, UV curing, fluorescence detection
- package and selection guidance: SMBB, EDC, EDCC, narrowband vs broadband
- Marubeni manufacturer authority and distributor positioning

## SEO Priorities

- build the two missing pillar pages: UV LED Guide and Multispectral LED Systems
- strengthen the three existing pillars: NIR LED Guide, LED Wavelength Guide, and IR LED vs Normal LED
- close the blog-to-product link gap so wavelength articles route buyers into the WooCommerce catalog
- fix broken datasheet PDF links on non-blog pages (collection and PDP layer)
- expand wavelength-specific landing pages for queries already ranking in position 5-9
- use DataForSEO to confirm intent and impression volume on wavelength and application queries

## AEO Priorities

- add spec tables to every PDP (peak wavelength, FWHM, forward voltage, forward current, viewing angle, package, radiant intensity / luminous flux)
- add FAQ blocks to every wavelength pillar and comparison article
- add direct-answer summary blocks at the top of wavelength selection articles
- build comparison content that explicitly answers "which wavelength for [application]"
- mark up FAQs, products, and articles with appropriate schema

## GEO Priorities

- present the Marubeni manufacturer relationship clearly across the site, schema, and entity profile
- build domain credibility in fNIRS, medical, and machine vision communities through application-specific content
- earn citations from technical reference sources where industrial LED selection is discussed (Reddit r/photonics, r/AskElectronics, Stack Exchange Electronics, Wikipedia LED articles)
- publish original wavelength selection guidance, package selection rationale, and application case studies that AI systems can cite
- track AI recommendations for technical buyer prompts (fNIRS, hyperspectral imaging, pulse oximetry, machine vision LED selection)
