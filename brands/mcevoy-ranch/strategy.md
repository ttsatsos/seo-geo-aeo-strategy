# McEvoy Ranch Search Strategy

## Strategic Goal

Make McEvoy Ranch easy for search engines and AI systems to find, understand, trust, cite, and recommend for premium olive oil, California food gifts, estate-grown products, and related culinary education.

## Priority Themes

- estate-grown California olive oil
- premium olive oil gifts
- corporate gifting
- holiday and host gifts
- recipes and usage education
- olive oil quality, storage, harvest, and flavor
- California / Petaluma / Marin-Sonoma provenance
- sustainable agriculture and ranch story

## SEO Priorities

- strengthen collection and PDP metadata
- build internal links from recipes/guides to products
- create comparison and gift-guide pages
- improve schema across products, FAQs, recipes, and organization/entity pages
- identify high-intent gaps with DataForSEO

## AEO Priorities

- add direct answer blocks to educational pages
- build concise FAQs for product, gifting, usage, storage, and shipping questions
- use comparison tables for gift guides and oil selection
- create "best for" sections on collection and guide pages

## GEO Priorities

- strengthen entity profile for McEvoy Ranch
- connect brand to real people, place, production methods, awards, reviews, and press
- improve PDP completeness and freshness
- build comparison pages that explain why/when to choose McEvoy products
- track AI share of voice for gift and olive oil recommendation prompts
