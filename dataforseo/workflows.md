# DataForSEO Workflows

## Keyword Expansion

Use seed keywords from each brand's `keyword-universe.md` to generate:

- related keywords
- question keywords
- long-tail commercial queries
- comparison queries
- "best X for Y" queries
- price-constrained queries

## SERP Feature Tracking

Track:

- organic rank
- featured snippets
- People Also Ask
- shopping modules
- local packs
- video/image results
- AI Overview presence
- AI Overview cited sources where available

## AI Overview / AI Mode Tracking

For priority prompts and queries:

- detect whether AI answer appears
- capture cited domains
- capture cited URLs
- capture answer text summary
- compare against competitor set
- identify missing evidence on owned pages

## Reporting Output

Store reports as CSV or Markdown summaries:

- query
- brand
- page URL
- date
- surface
- rank or citation status
- competitors cited
- recommendation
- next action
